Build bullet proof databases

Blockaudit is a decentralized platform that allows you to create databases. It does all the dirty work for you by automatically setting up blockchain based backend service allowing you to focus on what matters.

This DAPP will be built on the Cardano blockchain, the first blockchain platform to evolve out of a scientific philosophy and a research-first driven approach. It was developed by a large global collective of expert engineers and researchers.

This will enable developers to create records of: bank statements, medical history, investment performance, legislation, goverment budgets and even historical events and dates audited and verified on a public ledger. Once the records are created, they can't be edited or manipulated. All done without a third party that has to be trusted.

The project is open source based, ready for contributions from great minds across the globe.

Create a Decentralized Future

Audit your investment performance yourself

Create a trackrecord that can not be edited, manipulated or simply undermined by a larger fund with a better legal infrastructure. With the help of blockchain technology you can create a transparent and detailed investment record without a central authority proving that the transactions that make it up even exist.

You can now show off your investing skills which you spent thousands upon thousands of hours perfecting directly to your current and potential investors. Using smart contracts your investors will be able to keep your accountible as they will be able to see every move you make as it happens, in stead of finding about it the next quarter.

Own your medical records, make your doctor own his

What if you could see every patient's post-treatment condition of the therapist you're about to visit on a public ledger?

What if these patients would be happy to provide that information, because their personal data is secure and encrypted and the only thing they leave out public is the data that helps make lives better for everyone?

What if before you lay down that surgeon's operating table, you could now how many surgeries performed by him were successfull?

Using blockaudit.io platform, medical professionals will be able to provide you with more information and resouces than ever before. While the process on their end is as easy as scrolling through their favourite social media platform.

Create a democratic autonomous organization

Now that you have developed your idea and secured funds, what’s next? You have to hire managers, find a trustworthy CFO to handle the accounts, run board meetings and do a bunch of paperwork.

Or you can simply leave all that to an Ethereum contract. It will collect proposals from your backers and submit them through a completely transparent voting process.

One of the many advantages of having a robot run your organization is that it is immune to any outside influence as it’s guaranteed to execute only what it was programmed to. And because the Ethereum network is decentralized, you'll be able to provide services with a 100% uptime guarantee.
